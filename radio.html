Radios

I have a few hobbies, but as of late I have really been sinking time into HAM radios. For those of you who don't know, HAM radios (as opposed to normal radios) are just radios which operate on a frequency which HAM operators are lisenced to use.

There is a saying among HAMs which is what HAM means. It is actually an acryonym, and most HAMs agree that it stands for "Had Alot of Money." This is because HAM radios are expensive, really expenseive, in fact, they are too expensive. A good radio rig will run for about anywhere from $200.00 to the nice and capable rigs around $2,000.00



By the way, AM and FM are not frequencies, they are modes of transmission. Please don't make the same mistake that everyone else makes.
